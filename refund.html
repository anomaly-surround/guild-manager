Refund Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

7-Day Refund Guarantee

We want you to be happy with Guild Manager. If the service doesn't work as described, you can request a full refund within 7 days of your purchase date.

How to Request a Refund

Contact us via the Discord community or GitHub with your purchase details. We'll process your refund as quickly as possible.

Processing

All refunds are processed through Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Once approved, refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days depending on your payment method.

Monthly Subscriptions

If you cancel your monthly subscription, you will not be charged again. You'll retain Premium access until the end of your current billing period. Partial-month refunds are not provided for cancellations after the 7-day window.

Lifetime Purchases

Lifetime purchases are eligible for a refund within 7 days of purchase. After 7 days, lifetime purchases are non-refundable.

Exceptions

Refunds may be denied if there is evidence of abuse, such as repeated purchases and refund requests.

Contact

For refund requests, reach out via the Discord community or GitHub.
